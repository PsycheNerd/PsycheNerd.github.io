Symptoms may be

Positive Symptoms                                  Negative Symptoms
  • hallucinations (tactile, auditory, visual, olfactory and gustatory)
  • delusions
  • disordered thoughts and speech
  • Apathy
  • Absent or blunted emotional responses
  • Social withdrawal
  • Anhedonia
  • Lethargy

So far, the cause of schizophrenia is unknown, but there seems to be a genetic link.
Developing schizophrenia becomes more likely for each family member that has dealt with this illness and triggers may be drug or excessive alcohol consumption.


Possible Treatment

Medical treatment should include the intake of individual tolerable neuroleptics to reduce positive symptoms.
Also, social skills training is an aspect that shouldn't be neglected.
